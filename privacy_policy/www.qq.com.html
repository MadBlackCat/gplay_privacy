【十】如何设置朋友圈照片查看权限？ 10. How to manage the permission of viewing the WeChat Moments photos?

关于朋友圈查看权限，有以下几种情况： The followings are permissions that you can manage to set for WeChat Moments:

  • 未设置任何权限 No Restrictions

    默认公开，你的所有朋友，可以查看到你在朋友圈发表的所有照片。 The Default setting is public to all: all of your WeChat friends can review your posts in Moments.

  • 设置回避的人 Set “Share to”

    发布照片时，可设置“谁可以看”： You can set “Share to” before posting anything on Moments

    公开：所有朋友可见； All: all contacts can see;

    私密：仅自己可见； Private: just you can see;

    部分可见：可根据通讯录分组，选择可见的分组朋友； Share to Just selected contacts: select groups in contacts and only the selected contacts can see;

    不给谁看：可根据通讯录分组，选择不可见的分组朋友。 Don’t Share: select groups in contacts and the selected contacts will be excluded from reviewing your Moments posts.

  • 允许陌生人查看十张照片 Make Last 10 Posts Public

    陌生人（附近的人、摇一摇交友场景）只能看到10张最近发的照片。 A stranger (people nearby or friends by shaking) can only review the last 10 posts.

  • 朋友圈查看范围： Moments View Permission:

    可选择允许朋友查看朋友圈最近三天、最近半年或者全部的内容。 You can select “Last 3 days”, “Last 6 months” or “All” to be viewable by friends.

  • 关闭朋友圈插件 Disable Moments

    之前通过朋友圈发表的照片，可在个人相册里查看。 You can still find your photos posted on Moments in your album after you disable Moments.
